## üì¢ Call for Papers

**EMPOWER 2026** bridges the gap between the machine learning community and the mobile systems community. 

Mobile and wireless networks are evolving toward highly heterogeneous ecosystems, encompassing smartphones, wearables, and dense edge infrastructures. Simultaneously, the integration of AI into these systems has triggered an **‚ÄúAI Compute-Energy Crisis,‚Äù** where the demands of modern models often exceed the thermal and energy limits of constrained hardware.

We explore the full lifecycle of mobile intelligence. We solicit contributions that span the spectrum from algorithmic innovations to real-world system deployments, organized into two distinct tracks.

### üéØ Workshop Tracks

To address the diverse challenges of the AI-Mobile ecosystem, EMPOWER 2026 welcomes submissions under two main tracks:

#### **Track 1: AI for Networks**
*Focusing on scalable, trustworthy intelligence to optimize non-stationary network conditions and next-generation connectivity.*
* **Topics:**
    * AI-native 6G architectures
    * Trustworthy network operations and automation
    * Intelligent spectrum management
    * AI for network security and resilience

#### **Track 2: Systems for AI**
*Focusing on lightweight architectures, brain-inspired computing, and intelligent AI workload scheduling to make intelligence viable on the edge.*
* **Topics:**
    * On-device Generative AI (GenAI)
    * Model compression and quantization
    * Brain-inspired computing and neuromorphic systems
    * Energy-efficient AI workload scheduling
    * Thermal-aware mobile computing

## üìù Submission Guidelines

We invite original research papers that have not been previously published and are not currently under review for publication. 

### **Page Limits**
* **Full Papers:** Up to **6 pages** (including references).
* **Position Papers:** Up to **4 pages** (including references).

### **Formatting Requirements**
All submissions must be provided in PDF format and follow the specific formatting guidelines of MobiSys 2026:
* **Font:** 10pt font (or larger).
* **Paper Size:** Letter-sized (8.5" by 11").
* **Layout:** Double-column.
    * Column width: 9.25" by 3.33"
    * Space between columns: 0.33"
* **Spacing:** Single-spaced.
* **Abstract:** Must contain fewer than 250 words.

### **LaTeX Template**
Submissions can use the [ACM Proceedings LaTeX Template](https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template). Authors remain responsible for checking that their resulting PDF meets the formatting specifications above.

### **Review Process**
EMPOWER 2026 follows a **double-blind** review process. 
* **Anonymization:** Authors must not reveal their identity in the submission. Please remove names, affiliations, and contact information from the PDF.
* **Citations:** Cite your own work in the third person (e.g., "Smith et al. showed..." rather than "We showed...").
